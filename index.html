The muslim culture

The Quran is the central religious text of Islam, which Muslims believe to be a revelation from God. It is widely regarded as the finest work in classical Arabic literature. The Quran is divided into chapters, which are then divided to verses The books of the quran are called surat.Muslim do things in order according to qur'an.
